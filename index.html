About Me

Hey! Lester here. I'm an enthusiast photographer based in Toronto, Canada.

Many people wonder why I haven't used Wix or Squarespace and opted for a tedious, time consuming html-coded website to showcase my portfolio. Often, my reply is two-fold: I'm a broke student that cannot be bothered to spend money on another subscription (pls hire me); I love to experiment and start passion projects that enable me to extend my knowledge and appreciation of the world. I dabble in many hobbies such as chess, origami, music, and photography, with my current passion being coding and blockchain technology.

I am available for Portrait, Lifestyle, and Concert shoots in Toronto and the GTA. Feel free to browse my portfolio to get a sense of my framing and editing style. I'm a harmless and short Filipino guy, so don't hesitate and reach out through my socials if you have any inquiries or would like to chat about my hobbies!

My work is frequently featured on Punching Twins and Headway, indie rock bands based in Toronto, Canada. I also have works published in the independant student newspaper, The Eyeopener.
